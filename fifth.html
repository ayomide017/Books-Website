Totally Bored Boris


Description


What do you do when you are totally bored and don't feel like doing any activity? How do you kill the boredom? Totally Bored Boris is a short eBook story of a bear who was so bored to do anything. It was a lazy day and Boris, the bear felt so much boring. He lost interest in playing with toys, watching television, read all the books he had, and found nothing to kill his boredom.


Author: Hans Wilhelm

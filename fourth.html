Ten Little Bunnies


Description


There were bunnies in pink, orange, yellow, brown, white, grey, blue and many colors. The story is narrated with rhythmic words and fancy words, gives an enjoyable read. Swash, Swoop, Hoop, Boom, Heeelp, Sweep - these fancy words adds more zest to read the eBook!

The interesting part of the story is colorful pictures and illustrations. Young kids can easily follow the eBook and enjoy reading.

It is a simple funny rhyme illustrated through colorful painted images.

You can ask your child to look at the pictures and explain the story. Don't miss giving a read, rather just a glance!


Author: Nurit Karlin
